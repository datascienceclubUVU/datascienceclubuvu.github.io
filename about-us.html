ABOUT US

We are a student-run club at Utah Valley University who are obsessed with all things Data Science. This club provides students with opportunities to work on real-world projects, network with industry experts, and collaborate with other students. We primarily focus on the following areas:

Data Science | Data Engineering | Software Engineering | Data Analytics
