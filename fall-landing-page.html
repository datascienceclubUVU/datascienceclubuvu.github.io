Master Classes

  • Generative AI
  • Computer Vision

GCP Sandboxes

Description:
Pluralsight, a Utah-based online learning platform, has partnered with both UVU and Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to provide UVU students FREE access to all available GCP labs via the PluralSight platform. Why does this matter? PluralSight is typically $300 a year, and GCP's interactive labs are $30 a month. Thus, just by being a UVU student, you automatically save at least $42 a month, so take advantage of them!

How to Access the Sandboxes:

  1. Click here.
  2. Click the button that says "Pluralsight Site".
  3. Login using your UVU credentials.
  4. Once signed in, click the Browse tab.
  5. On the left side of the screen, under the Topic filter, select "Google Cloud Platform".
  6. Under the Course Type filter, select "Labs".
  7. Choose your desired sandbox environment and follow the instructions.
